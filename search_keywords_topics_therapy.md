## 三、實用延伸搜尋寫法

### 1. 查特定議題的治療效果
- `complex PTSD AND psychotherapy`
- `dissociative disorder AND treatment`
- `postpartum adjustment AND counseling`
- `suicide bereavement AND intervention`

### 2. 查特定療法應用在哪個族群或議題
- `EMDR AND complex trauma`
- `schema therapy AND personality disorder`
- `DBT AND suicide prevention`
- `mindfulness AND insomnia`
- `art therapy AND cancer patients`

### 3. 查系統性回顧或統合分析
- `PTSD AND EMDR AND systematic review`
- `couple therapy AND meta-analysis`
- `ADHD AND mindfulness AND review`
- `grief therapy AND systematic review`

### 4. 查兒童青少年族群
- `school refusal AND adolescents`
- `autism spectrum disorder AND children AND intervention`
- `play therapy AND children`
- `early intervention AND developmental delay`
